Rules
  • Setup
  • -Enter the number of players in the settings. Get number button will not work otherwise.
  • -Have a drinking cup in the middle of the table or players. This will be "The King's cup"
  • -Each player need to be numbered. You can write it or memorize it.
  • Rules
  • -Players will decide who will start the round. Player will press "get card" button to draw a card.
  • -Player who pressed the button will announce the order correlated to the card.
  • -If the order is to punish random player, "Get Number" button shall be pressed and the order will announce who will be punished with their number.
